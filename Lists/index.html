Steps to make bread

  1. Activate The Yeast (if using active dry yeast)
    • Dissolve the yeast and pinch of sugar in lukewarm water.
    • Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy, indicating that the yeast is active.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  3. Combine Wet Ingredients
    • If you didn't use the yeast activation step, simply add the yeast to the dry ingredients.
    • Add any sweetener (sugar or honey), olive oil, and water.
  4. Knead the Dough
    • Mix the wet and dry ingredients until a shaggy dough forms.
    • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead it for about 10-15 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. First Rise
    • PLace the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise until it doubles in size (usually 1-2 hours).
  6. Punch Down and Shape
    • Gently deflate the risen dough and shape it into your desired form (loaf, boule, etc.).
  7. Second Rise
    • Cover the shaped dough and let it rise again until its puffy and has increased in size (usually 30 minutes to an hour)
  8. Preheat the Oven
    • Preheat your oven to the desired temperature (usually 375-450°F or 190-230°C).
  9. Baking
    • If using a pan, place the dough in the greased or parchment-lined pan.
    • If using a baking sheet, you can bake the bread directly on the sheet.
    • Bake for 20-40 minutes, depending on the size and type of bread, until it's golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.
  10. Cooling
    • Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack for at least 30 minutes before slicing.
