7-Day Siesta Key Itinerary

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive in Siesta Key, check into your beachfront accommodation.
  • Afternoon: Relax and unwind on the renowned Siesta Key Beach, known for its powdery white sands.
  • Evening: Dinner at a beachside restaurant in Siesta Village.

Day 2

  • Morning: Explore Siesta Village for breakfast and shopping.
  • Afternoon: Rent a bike or take a leisurely stroll to discover the island's neighborhoods.
  • Evening: Sunset dinner cruise departing from nearby Sarasota.

Day 3

  • Morning: Visit Point of Rocks for snorkeling and exploring the unique limestone formations.
  • Afternoon: Kayaking or paddleboarding through the mangrove tunnels.
  • Evening: Dinner at a seafood restaurant overlooking the Gulf.

Day 4

  • Full Day: Take a day trip to Sarasota; visit the Marie Selby Botanical Gardens and Mote Marine Laboratory & Aquarium.
  • Evening: Dinner at a fine dining restaurant in downtown Sarasota.

Day 5

  • Morning: Head to Crescent Beach for a more secluded beach experience.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy beachside activities or simply relax.
  • Evening: Sunset picnic on the beach.

Day 6

  • Morning: Parasailing adventure over the Gulf of Mexico.
  • Afternoon: Jet skiing or other water sports activities.
  • Evening: Dinner at a casual beachfront eatery.

Day 7

  • Morning: Visit Turtle Beach for a serene morning.
  • Afternoon: Final beach relaxation and souvenir shopping.
  • Evening: Farewell dinner at a waterfront restaurant.
