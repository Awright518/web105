Destin-Fort Walton

Things to do

  • Relax on the Beach: Enjoy the pristine sands and emerald waters of Destin's beautiful beaches.
  • Explore Okaloosa Island: Discover the laid-back charm, fishing pier, and Gulfarium Marine Adventure Park.
  • Visit the Emerald Coast Science Center: Engage in interactive exhibits for a family-friendly learning experience.
  • Crab Island: Experience this unique submerged sandbar for boating, swimming, and socializing.
  • Take a Helicopter Tour: Gain a bird's-eye view of the stunning coastline and emerald waters.

5-Day Destin-Fort Walton Itinerary

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive in Fort Walton Beach, check-in at a beachfront resort.
  • Afternoon: Explore Okaloosa Island, visit the Gulfarium Marine Adventure Park.
  • Evening: Sunset dinner at a waterfront restaurant on the Destin Harbor Boardwalk.

Day 2

  • Morning: Relax on the sugar-white sands of Destin's Henderson Beach State Park.
  • Afternoon: Water activities like jet skiing or parasailing.
  • Evening: Seafood feast at a beachside eatery.

Day 3

  • Morning: Dolphin cruise for marine encounters.
  • Afternoon: Crab Island experience for boating and socializing.
  • Evening: Sunset helicopter tour for panoramic views.

Day 4

  • Morning: Visit Big Kahuna's Water & Adventure Park.
  • Afternoon: Shopping at Destin Commons.
  • Evening: Casual dinner at a family-friendly restaurant.

Day 5

  • Morning: Explore Fort Walton Beach's historic sites like the Indian Temple Mound Museum.
  • Afternoon: Lunch at a local cafe.
  • Evening: Departure, reflecting on the coastal memories.
