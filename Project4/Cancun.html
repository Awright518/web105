Things to do

  • Relax on Playa Delfines: Enjoy the white sands and turquoise waters of one of Cancun's most beautiful beaches.
  • Snorkel or Dive in the Great Maya Reef: Discover vibrant marine life in the world's second-largest coral reef.
  • Explore Xcaret Park: Experience nature, culture, and adventure in this ecological park.
  • Enjoy Water Activities: Parasailing, jet-skiing, and paddleboarding in the Caribbean Sea.
  • Explore the Underwater Museum (MUSA): Snorkel or dive among submerged sculptures creating an art-meets-nature experience.

8-Day Cancun Itinerary

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive in Cancun, check-in and relax
  • Afternoon: Explore Hotel Zone, lunch by the beach
  • Evening: Dinner at a seafood restaurant

Day 2

  • Morning: Visit Chichen Itza for a guided tour
  • Afternoon: Return to Cancun, relax by the pool/beach
  • Evening: Dine at a Mexican cuisine restaurant

Day 3

  • Morning: Snorkel or dive in the Great Maya Reef.
  • Afternoon: Leisure time for beach activities or spa relaxation.
  • Evening: Sunset catamaran cruise with dinner.

Day 4

  • Morning: Day trip to Isla Mujeres for beach exploration.
  • Afternoon: Try local specialties at island restaurants.
  • Evening: Return to Cancun for a relaxing night.

Day 5

  • Morning: Explore the Underwater Museum (MUSA) through snorkeling.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy lunch at a beachfront restaurant.
  • Evening: Attend a Lucha Libre show for an entertaining night.

Day 6

  • Morning: Experience Xcaret Park with its ecological and cultural attractions.
  • Afternoon: Relax and rejuvenate at the park's spa facilities.
  • Evening: Dinner at one of the on-site restaurants.

Day 7

  • Morning: Take a Cenote Tour for a unique natural adventure.
  • Afternoon: Free time for shopping at Mercado 28.
  • Evening: Farewell dinner at a seaside restaurant.

Day 8

  • Morning: Departure, with time for last-minute beach relaxation.
  • Afternoon: Check-out and transfer to the airport.
