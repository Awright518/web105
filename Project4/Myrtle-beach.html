6-Day Myrtle Beach Itinerary

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive in Myrtle Beach, check into your oceanfront hotel.
  • Afternoon: Stroll along the Myrtle Beach Boardwalk and Promenade, enjoy lunch at a beachside cafe.
  • Evening: Experience the Myrtle Beach SkyWheel for stunning views, followed by dinner at a seafood restaurant.

Day 2

  • Morning: Relax on Myrtle Beach State Park, enjoy beach activities.
  • Afternoon: Visit Broadway at the Beach for shopping, dining, and attractions like Ripley's Aquarium.
  • Evening: Dinner at an entertainment complex restaurant.

Day 3

  • Morning: Explore Brookgreen Gardens, featuring sculpture gardens, botanical landscapes, and a Lowcountry zoo.
  • Afternoon: Lunch at a garden cafe, followed by leisure time.
  • Evening: Sunset dinner at Murrells Inlet MarshWalk.

Day 4

  • Morning: Family fun at Family Kingdom Amusement Park and Splashes Oceanfront Water Park.
  • Afternoon: Relax at the beach or hotel pool.
  • Evening: Pirate Voyage Dinner & Show for an entertaining dinner experience.

Day 5

  • Morning: Tee off at one of Myrtle Beach's renowned golf courses.
  • Afternoon: Visit Huntington Beach State Park and Atalaya Castle.
  • Evening: Dinner at a local seafood restaurant.

Day 6

  • Morning: Enjoy Myrtle Waves Water Park for thrilling water slides and pools.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Myrtle Beach Art Museum.
  • Evening: Farewell dinner at a favorite local spot.
