Things to do

  • Hiking in the Santa Ynez Mountains: Explore scenic trails like Inspiration Point or Lizard's Mouth for breathtaking views.
  • Santa Barbara Funk Zone: Immerse yourself in the vibrant arts district with murals, galleries, and trendy wine-tasting rooms.
  • Santa Barbara Mission: Visit the iconic Old Mission Santa Barbara, a historic Spanish mission with beautiful gardens.
  • Wine Tasting in Santa Ynez Valley: Take a day trip to the nearby wine country for vineyard tours and wine tasting.
  • Lotusland: Discover the botanical wonders of Lotusland, a unique garden estate with rare plants and sculptures.

4-Day Santa Barbara Itinerary

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive in Santa Barbara, check into your hotel.
  • Afternoon: Explore Stearns Wharf, enjoy lunch with ocean views.
  • Evening: Discover the vibrant Santa Barbara Funk Zone for art, wine-tasting, and dinner.

Day 2

  • Morning: Visit the Santa Barbara Courthouse, climb the clock tower for panoramic views.
  • Afternoon: Explore Lotusland, a unique botanical garden estate.
  • Evening: Dinner at a downtown restaurant.

Day 3

  • Morning: Hike in the Santa Ynez Mountains, perhaps to Inspiration Point.
  • Afternoon: Day trip to Santa Ynez Valley for wine tasting and vineyard tours.
  • Evening: Sunset dinner at a winery or return to downtown Santa Barbara.

Day 4

  • Morning: Relax on Butterfly Beach, enjoy the seaside ambiance.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Santa Barbara Zoo or the Sunken Gardens at the Courthouse.
  • Evening: Farewell dinner at a harbor-side restaurant.
